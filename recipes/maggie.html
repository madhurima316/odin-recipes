maggie



Description

Magie is an instant noodle .


Ingredients

  • water
  • maggie noodle
  • veggies
  • maggie masala

Steps

  1. boil the water
  2. add the noodles
  3. after some time, add masala and fried vegetables
  4. after 2-3 minutes , it is ready to be served

    5. Maggie is ready!
